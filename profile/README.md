Welcome to **GeofastGames** - where strategy meets geography in fast-paced mobile gaming!

## About Us

GeofastGames is dedicated to creating engaging, educational, and strategic mobile games that bring the world together. Our flagship title, **Geofast: Battle of Nations**, combines geographical knowledge with real-time multiplayer strategy, creating a unique gaming experience that's both fun and enriching.

## Our Games

### Geofast: Battle of Nations
A real-time multiplayer battle strategy game where players represent nations in strategic warfare.

**Features:**
- Real-time multiplayer battles
- 40+ supported languages
- Strategic weapon systems
- Offline and online game modes
- Cross-platform mobile support

**Tech Stack:**
- Frontend: Godot Engine 4.5
- Backend: Nakama Go Server
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Analytics: Firebase
